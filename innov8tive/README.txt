<strong>INSTALL</strong>
1. Upload the Innovative child theme folder via FTP to your wp-content/themes/ directory. The Genesis parent theme needs to be in the wp-content/themes/ directory as well.
2. Go to your WordPress dashboard and select Appearance.
3. Activate the Innovative theme.
4. Inside your WordPress dashboard, go to Genesis > Theme Settings and configure them to your liking.

<strong>WIDGET AREAS</strong>
Primary Sidebar - This is the primary sidebar if you are using the Content/Sidebar or the Sidebar/Content Site Layout option.
Secondary Sidebar - This is the sidebar-alt.
Home Top - This is the top section of the homepage and is great for a featured post slider.
Home Left - This is left section under home top and is a great place for a photo and bio.
Home Right - This is the right section under home top and can you used to feature desired content.
Home Bottom - This is the bottom section under home top and is the perfect place to feature your blog posts.
Footer #1 - This is the first column of the footer section.
Footer #2 - This is the second column of the footer section.
Footer #3 - This is the third column of the footer section.
Footer #4 - This is the third column of the footer section.

<strong>FEATURED IMAGES</strong>
By default WordPress will create a default thumbnail image for each image you upload and the size can be specified in your dashboard under Settings > Media. In addition, the Innovative child theme creates the following thumbnail images you'll see below, which are defined (and can be modified) in the functions.php file. These are the recommended thumbnail sizes that are used on the Innovative child theme demo site.

home-bottom -     	150px by 225px
home-middle -     	265px by 150px
home-mini - 		80px by 80px
home-supermini -	65px by 45px
primary-sidebar -	290px by 150px

<strong>Color Style Options</strong>
You have three Color Styles to choose from.  These can be chosen on the Genesis Theme Setting page under the header "Color Style"

Green & Blue (default)
Turquoise & Red
Yellow & Orange
